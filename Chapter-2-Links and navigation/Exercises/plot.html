Plot

Kevin, who is suffering from dissociative identity disorder has 23 alter egos, all extremely different from one another, kidnaps three teenagers. They must figure out his friendly personas before he unleashes his hidden 24th personality which threatens their life dominating the other personas.

Cast

Split is a 2016 American psychological thriller film written, directed and produced by M. Night Shyamalan, starring James McAvoy as Kevin Wendell, Anya Taylor-Joy as Casey Cookie, Betty Buckley as Dr. Karen Fletcher, and Jessica Sula as Marcia.
